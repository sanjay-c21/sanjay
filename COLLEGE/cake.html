TO BAKE A CAKE

  • ingredients:
    1. 1 cup white sugar
    2. half cup unsalted butter
    3. 2 large eggs
    4. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    5. 2 teaspoons baking powder
    6. half cup milk
  • procedure:
    1. proheat the oven to 350degrees F
    2. grease and flour a inch square cake pan
    3. cream the sugar and butter
    4. combine flour and bakingg powder
    5. pour cake batter into the prepared pan
    6. bake cake in the preheated oven
    7. remove from the oven and cool completely
